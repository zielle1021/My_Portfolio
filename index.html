My career goals and aspirations

Short-term Goals

As a third-year BSIT student, my immediate goal is to secure an internship or junior remote position in UI/UX design to gain practical experience in the tech industry. I plan to master Figma prototyping and develop at least three interactive design projects to enhance my personal portfolio. Additionally, I aim to give back by organizing a coding workshop or webinar for high school students in my community, helping them explore opportunities in technology. To broaden my exposure and professional network, I also intend to attend at least three local or virtual technology conferences, such as DevCon PH, Graphika Manila, or UX+ Conference.

Mid-term Vision

Over the next two to three years, I aspire to lead a capstone or research project focusing on smart city infrastructure or Internet of Things (IoT) solutions, aligning with current global trends in innovation. I aim to contribute to open-source initiatives that promote ethical AI development and begin to establish myself as an enthusiast or emerging expert in areas like quantum computing or cybersecurity. Participating in innovation challenges or tech hackathons is also part of my plan, with the goal of earning recognition for creative and impactful solutions.

Long-term Aspirations

In the long run, I envision launching a tech startup or non-profit organization that focuses on clean technology or widening access to IT education. I hope to contribute to the development of advanced software solutions for emerging fields such as quantum computing, and champion sustainable tech practices by promoting modular device designs that help reduce electronic waste. Ultimately, I aim to be recognized as a thought leader who effectively bridges the gap between cutting-edge technology and meaningful social impact, using my skills to create a better, more equitable future.
